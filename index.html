About Me

How
I Started

My parents opened a business computer related when I was 5 years old. After school I used to spend hours in paint and Print Master designing stuff. The time past and as I graduated from high school my parents also close their shop. While I was in University wondering around what to do with my life I took a Macrame class(for University credit :D). I loved it, it was my favorite thing to do. I also used to watch HGTV non stop(house renovation and interior design for days). But as society believes now day(or at least then) they are just hobbies. I needed to choose a degree and start taking "real classes".

I then went to study a BSc in Speech and Language Therapy. Guess what? My favorite part was creating the material for the kids therapies. After a year working as a SLT I needed a new challenge so decided to become an Audiologist. Long story short, moved to the United Kingdom to study and now I have an MSc in Audiology. Now 2 years later after practicing as an Audiologist I started to find my passion for art and design again. I took a Web Development class by Code First Girl(they do it all over the UK) and it was just the kick I needed. Ever since I've been craving more. I decided to star teaching my self how to use Illustrator and practice more coding and web design. Hence, how this portfolio started. Here I will upload my work for people to see and for me to keep track and see the timeline of my improvement.
